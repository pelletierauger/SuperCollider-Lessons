SuperCollider lesson plan

- Introduction to the language and the client-server structure.
- Creating basic synthesizers.
- Composing patterns with synthesizers and the pattern library.
- Envelopes and multichannel expansion.
- Methods of synthesis : 
  - Additive synthesis.
  - Substractive synthesis.
  - Frequency modulation (FM).
  - Sampling (working with sound files and buffers).
  - Granular synthesis.
  - Modal synthesis (physical modeling).
- Clocks and event scheduling.
- Strategies for composing and performing complete pieces.
